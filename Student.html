Student's Club

About

If a 40-inch LCD television combined with huge space for pool , billiards and snookers tables, didn't make it the best hangout place on the campus , the student's club now also boasts 2 play station 4 sets. Indoor games like chess , carom, foosball and table tennis are also available here. The open space in front of the club, called the UG floor , serves as the most happening place durin the time of THOMSO and COGNIZANCE. It also serves as a gathering place during Holi and Diwali.
