LECTURE HALL COMPLEX

About

The Lecture Hall Complex, constructed by NBCC for IIT Roorkee has been declared "Outstanding Concrete Structure of Uttarakhand" for the year 2010 by India Concrete Institute.
The design focus here has been to make maximum use of natural daylight and ventilation, with the building orientation planned to complement it. This complex follows the Modern Style of Architecture which is seen and followed by most of the buildings in the campus. The built-up area of the complex is designed to be 1,43,000 Sq. Ft.
